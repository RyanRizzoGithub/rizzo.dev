About Me

Hi, I'm Ryan Rizzo — a computer science graduate, builder of quirky ideas, and lifelong explorer of the things that make me curious. Welcome to my digital garden..

I'm someone who likes to learn by doing. Over the years that's taken the form of writing clean code, reverse-engineering problems just to understand how they tick, creating generative art from mathematical patterns, composing music, experimenting with game design, and even building physical projects like tank scapes and Arduino devices. If something catches my imagination, I almost always end up turning it into a project.

I studied Computer Science at the University of Arizona, graduating in December 2023, and I've worked across a wide range of environments from technical roles in software and data, to coaching and leadership development in behavioral-health settings, to esports team leadership. That blend of experiences taught me how to communicate clearly, stay adaptable, and collaborate with people from all kinds of backgrounds.

This site is where I share what I learn along the way — projects, notes, experiments, and ideas that help me think out loud.

Whether you're here for my programming work, my creative projects, or just browsing, thanks for stopping by. And if anything I'm working on resonates with you, feel free to reach out — I love meeting other builders and curious minds.
